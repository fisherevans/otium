These all cover the same feature set — weight (very low → favorite), per-source cap, feed membership, add/archive/edit, sort, and feed-health insight — but they organize it around different ideas. Every preview is live; tap it to open full-screen and actually change a weight, sort, or archive something. All grayscale, so weight and noise read through ink density and marks, not color.

How to read this. The through-line is turn down, don't cut off — so pay attention to how each makes reweighting feel, since that's the gesture you'll do most. You can also mix ("the health dashboard up top, the expandable list below," or "the mixing-board faders inside the detail sheet"). And these answer the open questions in the feature doc differently — e.g. buckets vs faders vs steppers for weight.
